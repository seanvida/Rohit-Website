5. Amore de Cinque-Terre

Cinque Terre! I still feel nostalgic for my time there! The assimilation of 5 villages: Monterosso al Mare, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola, and Riomaggiore. The coastline, the five villages, and the surrounding hillsides are all part of the Cinque Terre National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
