Philosophy of life-
I belive the end purpose of everything is happiness. Happiness is what leads to contentment and contentment gives you a sense of balance and wellbeing. For me happiness comes from harmony in the following pursuits: Health, Finances, Relationships, Career, Learning. As long as all the things are equally prioritised, there is no reason of seeking happiness elsewhere.
